The Great Masters


We believe that human rights of a child are non-negotiable


We’re a school that believes that it’s more productive for young kids to focus on learning, developing skills, having fun playing sports, etc. than short-term results on a scoreboard.

Our Values


Our goal is to respond to the call of creating the emerging leaders of tomorrow. We are committed to impacting a synergy of skills, knowledge, and values in our children to lend them their inner voice for the 21st century.

MISSION: We envision today’s children as tomorrow’s leadership icons. We have impacted a milestone departure from the teacher-led to the child-centric. Our learning environment enables each child to realize their unique learning style, while our MI-aided methodology helps them discover their own creative and aesthetic potential.


VISION: The mission of THE GREAT MASTER is to build rich, meaningful learning experiences using the expertise and potential of staff, students, and families. We seek to help each student achieve academic success and develop a lifelong love of learning. We believe that all students must discover their capacity and responsibility to make a positive contribution to the world around them.
